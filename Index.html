Introduction to Gaza’s Humanitarian Crisis

Gaza has endured relentless attacks, with thousands killed and entire communities displaced. Our goal is to shed light on these atrocities and mobilize global support.

Documented War Crimes

  • Baptist Hospital Bombing: A deadly airstrike claimed the lives of hundreds seeking refuge.
  • Destruction of Rafah Refugee Tents: Shelters for displaced families were set ablaze.
  • Targeting of Al-Shifa Medical Complex: Gaza’s largest medical facility was devastated.
  • White Phosphorus Attacks: Illegal chemical weapons used in civilian areas.
